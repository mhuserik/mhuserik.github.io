New names and faces making a beat

HOME ARTISTS PLACES TRENDING

Rezz

A Young Candian Artist making waves in the world of bass. Signed with Mau5trap and OWSLA, Rezz has been making a name for herself since 2015. From making music with The Glitch Mob, K?d and 1788-L Rezz has created a cult following with her unique style and solid production. Being one of the youngest artist at age 22, Rezz has already swept through festivals like Paradiso, EDC Las Vegas and Tomorrowlnd. She is a name to look out for and a act that completes every lineup.

Alison Wonderland

Hailing from Australia, Alison Wonderland sits on the thrown as one of the biggest female djs to date. Alison is one of the few djs that use their own vocals in their tracks. Her songs range from light and vocal heavy to beats with a dirty bass drop. Starting in 2015 she has become more and more popular with the EDM community. She recently released a new track with Dillion francis and will be on the watch list for years to come.

BONNIE X CLYDE

The killer duo based in sunny California has been making headway on through the charts with their lighthearted lyric. Always energenitc and clean on the stage makes their set one you don't want to miss. The two are fresh faces and have already made a mark as a sound you want to watch.

Drezo

A hard hitting sound that will make you want to jump. Drezos dark creations pair beautifuly with his even darker visuals. As a newer artist he has been recognized by names like Diplo, Porter Robinson, and Tchami. His innovative sounds have brought new innovation to the scene.

Tchami

Coming from France, Tchami has a been a titan of future house with some chart topping EDM. His creative twist on religious imagery is edgy and pushes the limit of the power of music at it's best. After touring with many of the other titans of music, he has come out with a packed a+ portfolio. His experimental work has broken ground for other artists keen to change the worlk of dance music.

Vini Vici

A duo from Israel that has mixed and old school sound and transformed it into a progressive psytrance piece of art. One of the most unique sounds out there these guys bring the dance to every show. Thier recent songs with tenured artist Armin Van Buren has brought them to many more ears and spreading the psytrance movement to the rest of the world.

JOYRYDE

The name says it all for this artist. His work has skyrocketed him to fame that some artists only dream about. His fast beats and fun mixes keep the crowd dancing from start to finish. His presence is felt in nightclubs as well as venues like Paradiso. This is one artist that won't be dropping off the charts anytime soon.

Anna Lunoe

Anna Lunoe sits as one of the power house women of edm sinking hits at the top of the charts. Yet another ausi artist to rise to fame through pure talent. With a backgorund of jobs in the music industry he music shows her musical knowledge through complexity and rythm. Her knowledge of the industry has shown greatly as she now hosts a weekly show with Apple and has played at festivals like Paradiso, Lollapalooza and Hard Summer. The performance to solidify her career took place when she became the first female artist to dj solo on the Electric Daisy Carnival main stage on June 2016. She has continued her career after announcing her pregnancy and continued to tour up and down the west coast in 2017.

Win and Woo

In the same family with artists like Odesza, Autograf, and Sam Feldt, Win and Woo have had big shows to fill. The two chicago djs make infectious light beats that make it hard not to dance to. While the majority of their work is remixes, the two manage to remake a popular song so well it almost seems original. Breaking into the scene with their own music Win and Woo are a team to watch out for at your next festival.

Griz

This saxophone weilding dj from Michigan has become a world wide sensation for his groovy tracks and unique sets. His genre electro-soul, is the best way to descibe his sound. With one set of deep rich tone griz will shake your soul and leave a lasting impression.

AC Slater

A bass-heavy hitter that has swept across the would and gained a massive following for his of his oldschool spice of synths an breaks. An encourager of house music put AC Slater at the for front of festivals and tour alike.

Liu

Liu stands as a deep house artist influence heavily by Alok. At a very young age he has kicked off his career and popularity accross many parts of South America. He tours frequently and plays with many big names further solidifying his career as an artist changing the sounds of EDM.

Alok

The son of some of the pioneers of psytrance, Alok does not disapoint. His Brazillian beats have a steady melodic flow while remaining intense. Starting in 2012 it is easy to see Alok has just begun to scratch the surface of sound.

What So Not

Another Australian artist making an impact on the future of EDM. After splitting from Flume, What So Not created his own unique sound and has continued to push the boundaries of the comeplexity of EDM. His hard melodramatic beats reach a spiritual message and shake you with a heavy handed beat back to reality.

KSHMR

Splitting off from his career in the Cataracs, KSHMR has shown his skills as a music producer as his solo act in electro-house. His first singles were released with other hard hitting EDM artists setting the stage for his success from the begining.

MOMENT

This up and coming artist from oregon is one to look out for. As a part of the massive EDM community he knows the sounds the crowd wants to hear. His dark tracks and heavy beats couple together to make his sets unique and fresh.

ODESZA

In anticipation of Odesza's up and coming festival Sundara, they are one crew that is alwasy on repeat. Hailing from the EDM community the duo has crossed the pop charts to make it big in the main stream. Their custom sounds and creative remixes calm the soul. This rare Seattle pair have created a cult following through orchastral sound scape and complex show production.

NERO

A Bristish trio well known for their early break into the world of EDM long before it gained it's presedence in popular culture. Their music lives in a unique space between drum and bass, french house and, dubstep. Nero had multiple show stopping spotlights on BBC radio and have continued to tour around the world with the newest and oldest acts in the business.

Mija

An artist from Pheonix Arizona who has built her image on care free vibes and a wildness that seeps into her music. As a down to earth artists she often ends up in the crowd dancing with fans enjoying the vibe just as much as everyone else attending. Her discovery in 2014 during a one off set with artist Skillex, is one of the greatest rises to power for a female dj in this century. Her music has not been limited to one genre and as she tours and releases new music it seems that trend is here ot stay for this wild unsigned artist.
