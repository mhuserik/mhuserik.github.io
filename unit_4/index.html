Rezz

A Young Candian Artist making waves in the world of bass. Signed with Mau5trap and OWSLA, Rezz has been making a name for herself since 2015. From making music with The Glitch Mob, K?d and 1788-L Rezz has created a cult following with her unique style and solid production. Being one of the youngest artist at age 22, Rezz has already swept through festivals like Paradiso, EDC Las Vegas and Tomorrowlnd. She is a name to look out for and a act that completes every lineup.

BONNIE X CLYDE

The killer duo based in sunny California has been making headway on through the charts with their lighthearted lyric. Always energenitc and clean on the stage makes their set one you don't want to miss. The two are fresh faces and have already made a mark as a sound you want to watch.

Alison Wonderland

Hailing from Australia, Alison Wonderland sits on the thrown as one of the biggest female djs to date. Alison is one of the few djs that use their own vocals in their tracks. Her songs range from light and vocal heavy to beats with a dirty bass drop. Starting in 2015 she has become more and more popular with the EDM community. She recently released a new track with Dillion francis and will be on the watch list for years to come.

Tomorrowland

July 19th-28th, 2019

Hosted in belgium, this festival takes the cake for one of the biggest EDM travel destinations. Tomorrowland was founded in 2005 and has continued to dazzel crowds with artistry lacking from other venues. With names like Amelie Lens, Black Coffee, Carl Cox, DJ Diesel, Don Diablo, Oliver Heldens, Steve Aoki and, The Chainsmokers the promise of one of the most unforgetable experiences seems to hold true!

Established in 2009 by Made Event, the internationally renowned Electric Zoo Festival is one of New York City’s largest music festivals and features the top names in electronic music, bringing a wide variety of acts from around the world and across the spectrum of electronic music’s various sub-genres.
